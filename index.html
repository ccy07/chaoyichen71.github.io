Hi! I am Chaoyi Chen!


I’m a PhD Candidate in the Rutgers Department of Linguistics.
I am interested in Syntax, Semantics, and their interfaces. Language-wise, I specialize in English, Mandarin Chinese and Fuzhou Chinese .
My dissertation investigates the syntax and semantics of free relatives, supervised by Mark Baker and Yimei Xiang.



Recent activities:

2023, January. Chinese verbal reduplication and the one-delimitation principle. Oral presentation at LSA’s 97th Annual Meeting. Denver, Colorado, USA.
