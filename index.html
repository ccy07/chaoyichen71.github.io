Hi! I am Chaoyi Chen!


I’m a second-year Ph.D. student in the Rutgers Department of Linguistics.
I am interested in Syntax, Semantics, and Syntax-Morphology interface.
Language-wise, I specialize in Mandarin Chinese and Fuzhou Chinese (a prestige variety of the Eastern Min Branch of Min Chinese), both of which are my native languages.
